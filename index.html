Our Story

An app with a calling.

"I looked at the exam. It was the exact same test everyone else received. The difference is that this kid barely spoke English."

One day, I walked into a classroom to support an EAL student. The teacher told me, "This kid did nothing on the exam." I sat next to him and asked one question: "If you could understand the language, could you do these exercises?"

He said yes.

Using my own trained AI, I created a differentiated assessment in two minutes. The child started working. Really working. Beautifully working.

The teacher was stunned. This kid understood the concepts all along. He just couldn't access them because of the language barrier.

And then it hit me: if I could help that student without even knowing the subject, imagine what the expert teacher could accomplish with the right tools in their hands.

This isn't one child's story. I've seen dozens like him. The shy ones. The ones who needed encouragement. The naughty ones. Kids sitting in classrooms, feeling segregated, convinced they were "stupid" because they "didn't get it."

I spent months teaching myself AI. I tried, I failed, I tried again. Until I found a way to remove the two biggest obstacles between teachers and truly inclusive classrooms: the need to master AI prompting, and the impossibility of differentiating for every single learner.

I didn't just build a tool. I replicated what I do every day as a passionate educator. I take what the teacher knows and transform it into exactly what the student needs. That's what Educator Companion™ does.

Educator Companion™ isn't just another AI platform.
It's an app that reflects the heart of true educators.

Across All Three Tiers

One platform. Every level of support.

Educator Companion™ works across the entire MTSS framework, from universal instruction to intensive intervention.

Tier 1 — Universal

Differentiation for all

Every teacher can create materials adapted to diverse learners. No more "one-size-fits-all" instruction.

Teachers use it for:

  • Scaffolded lesson plans by language proficiency
  • Tiered assignments and assessments
  • UDL-aligned activities with multiple entry points
  • Vocabulary-rich materials for multilingual learners
Tier 2 — Targeted

Small group interventions

Support teachers create student support plans and targeted materials for students who need additional scaffolding beyond Tier 1.

Support teams use it for:

  • Creating comprehensive student support plans through guided conversation
  • Push-in and pull-out session materials
  • Modified tasks aligned to IEP and support plan goals
  • Collaboration resources for co-teaching
Tier 3 — Intensive

Individual student support

Highly personalized materials for students with significant learning gaps or intensive intervention needs.

Specialists use it for:

  • Individualized materials matching specific goals
  • Adapted assessments with appropriate accommodations
  • Communication scaffolds for counselor collaboration
  • Parent-friendly reports and recommendations
